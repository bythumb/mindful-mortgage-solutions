About Bill

A career built on relationships, not transactions.

Bill's mortgage career began in Los Angeles in 2004, following two years as a Realtor in Seattle. What started as a profession quickly became a calling — guiding clients through home purchases, refinances, and the financial decisions that shape their lives.

Through every shift in the industry — rate cycles, regulation changes, market expansions and contractions — the work has remained the same: helping amazing individuals and families navigate the path to homeownership and being counted on as their trusted mortgage advisor.

"I find joy and purpose in the success and well-being of others — and a mortgage is one of the most meaningful tools a family has to build that."

Bill operates as Mindful Mortgage Solutions, a DBA of First Link Mortgage Inc., serving California with access to a wide range of conventional, government, specialty, and non-traditional loan programs.

Loan Programs

The right loan, for every chapter.

From first-time buyers to seasoned investors and retirees seeking equity, Bill offers a comprehensive portfolio of lending solutions through First Link Mortgage's wholesale network.

Conventional & Jumbo

Traditional financing for primary residences and high-value properties up to and beyond conforming limits.

FHA & VA

Government-backed programs with flexible qualifying terms — ideal for first-time buyers and veterans.

Renovation Loans

Combine purchase or refinance with renovation costs into a single loan — for fixers, additions, and remodels.

Reverse Mortgages

A specialty practice. Tap into your home equity for retirement income, healthcare, or peace of mind — without monthly mortgage payments.

Bill's Specialty
Non-QM (Self-Employed)

Alternative qualification paths for self-employed borrowers, foreign nationals, and unique credit profiles.

Bank Statement Loans

P&L-based qualifying and bank statement programs for borrowers without traditional W-2 income.

Asset Qualifying

Use liquid assets as the basis for qualifying — designed for high-net-worth borrowers and retirees.

Investment Property (DSCR)

Debt-service coverage ratio loans qualified on the property's rental income — for portfolio investors.

Construction & Hard Money

Fix & flip, ground-up construction, and short-term bridge financing for time-sensitive projects.

Reverse Mortgage Specialty

Your home equity, working for your retirement.

A reverse mortgage allows homeowners 62 and older to convert home equity into income — supplementing retirement, covering healthcare, eliminating monthly mortgage payments, or simply providing peace of mind. Bill has built a focused specialty practice around guiding seniors and their families through the decision with patience and clarity.
